BEB Agro

BEB Agro is an Argriculture Farm where we provide healthy milk, meat and farm animals to your door step.

Our Story

With an aim to provide, all over Bangladesh, safe organic healthy food we started our journey in 2010. Along the way we learned a lot about the food in Bangladesh and our love for our farm animals has only grown.

Our moto is to raise healty farm animals and to provide people the best in class meat and healthy animals for Qurbani.

Why BEB Agro ?

We grow food in our farm for our animals. We check weight every month. We are aware about our animals mental health and we let them freely walk around in the farm.

All our animals are vaccinated. We call vet in case of any need.
